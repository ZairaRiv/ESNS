About ESNS:
            The main purpose of this website to create a school notification system by using a web application that will allow you to be updated during school emergencies, particularly dealing with school shootings. Users can be made reports by easy way. Students can directly report by clicking report button that will automatically send the information to be processed without being obvious to the perpetrator. This will send a report out to admin to look over and to notify the police. Once police are notified, other students and administrators will also be notified and the location of danger will also be shown to the student.
